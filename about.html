ABOUT US

The Football Academy of Sierra Leone (FASL) provides an integrated pathway for elite youth football development. Our programmes combine structured training, player welfare, academic support and community engagement to build a strong future for Sierra Leonean football.

Partners & Sponsors

Our partners support training, infrastructure, scholarships and community programmes — ensuring long-term sustainability and opportunities for youth.

Personnel & Staff

Our team includes licensed coaches, analysts, medical staff and educators who ensure a professional environment for development.

Stadium Tours

Visitors can explore our facilities, training grounds and mini museum showcasing the history and achievements of Sierra Leone football.

History

Established to nurture the next generation of footballers, FASL has helped produce athletes who progressed to national and regional competitions.

Vision & Mission

Vision: To be the leading talent development centre in West Africa.
Mission: Provide elite coaching, welfare, education and opportunity.

Membership

Members support academy programmes and receive early access to events, discounts and exclusive updates.
